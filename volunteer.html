VOLUNTEER WITH FLY

Our fantastic network of volunteers has helped us achieve incredible results! Last year alone, 178 volunteers provided nearly 8,000 hours of service.

Interested in volunteering?

We are always looking for energetic individuals who are interested in becoming a mentor, facilitating a law/life skills class, or contributing special talents and skills like photography.

INVEST IN A FLY YOUTH: BE A MENTOR

  • Mentors are matched with a youth who is on probation and/or struggling with drug or alcohol use.
  • Mentors must be 21 years or older
  • Mentors meet with their mentees one-on-one weekly.
  • Mentors participate in free monthly activities with their mentees throughout the year, such as white water rafting, laser tag, ice skating, and a high ropes course.
  • Because building trust and sparking change takes time and consistency, mentors must be willing to make a 12-month commitment.

TEACH LEGAL EDUCATION

  • Teams of 2-4 facilitators teach FLY’s semester-long juvenile law and life skills class to youth who are on probation or at risk of becoming system involved.
  • Classes meet once a week for approximately 12 weeks.
  • No prior legal education or experience is necessary — FLY provides a well-developed curriculum and training.

How you can help

Spread the word about FLY by hosting a Why FLY event at your home. Why FLY events are intimate gatherings of 8-12 people interested in learning more about FLY and the youth we serve.

Host a Table our next fundraising event for FLY. Table Hosts invite friends, family, and coworkers interested in getting more involved in their community, and who care about giving youth a chance to become more than their past mistakes.
